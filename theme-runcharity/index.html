♥ Frequently Asked Questions ♥

Have a question about our clan? Check for an answer here! If you didn't find an answer, contact us through the form below!

How can I join your clan?

If you would like to join us, you should submit a membership application through the form below. See the question "What should my application contain?" for details on what an application should look like. When your application has been received, it will be reviewed by the members. If any family member disapproves of the new applicant, the application will be rejected. Families are serious business. :)

Does 'eS allow multi-clanning?

'eS does not allow multi-clanning. Multi-clanning reduces the loyalty to each clan and splits the interest of the member. This is also not good for training and competitive purposes. Sorry! :)

Are there any skill requirements to join?

There are no skill requirements to join 'eS. For members who are new to the game, we do encourage you to practice frequently, and we are always there to help you train. :)

What should my application contain?

Interested in joining us? Great! Your application should contain answers to the following:
  • Nickname
  • Other nicknames
  • Previous clans
  • Country
  • Age
  • Preferred maps
  • Preferred modes
  • Why do you want to join 'eS?
  • Why should we accept you into our family?
  • Do you use swear words online or in real life?
  • How long have you played Cube 2 Sauerbraten?
  • How much do you play each day/week?

Are you a mapping or fragging clan?

Our clan is centered around fragging, though we have some skilled mappers and coders as well.

Does 'eS play competitively?

We have not yet participated in Sauerbraten tournaments, though we clanwar often and hold little tournamemnts with ourselves :)

Are there any age restrictions?

There are no age restrictions at all our clan. We have members from 7 to 30 years old. :) We're a family, you see :D

Are there any activity requirements?

We don't have any requirements on play time, but we do ask members to keep in touch by Slack at least once a week, unless otherwise arranged.

What do you use for communication?

For internal communication, we use Slack, a messaging app for teams. It's a bit more serious than Discord and has a web client. We also have a public Discord channel, and a Steam group.

♥ We welcome new members! ♥

If you've read all about us and feel like you're a good fit for our team, send us an application! We hope to hear from you soon!
